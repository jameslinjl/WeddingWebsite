J & T

10.17.15

OUR STORY

Shortly after starting their freshman year at Columbia University, Tanni and James met for the first time. Both had gotten involved with Christian Union at Columbia, a ministry and family that would later evolve into Columbia Faith and Action, earlier in the semester, but at that point the men and the women in the group had not crossed the gender barrier and met each other yet. And so at a Christian Union social event in JJ's Place, the two were fatefully introduced... and did not speak a word to each other that night.

Their first conversation was prompted by Tanni (at the time called "Tatianna" by James) being the sole attendee of a praise and worship night hosted by James. After struggling through finding songs they both knew, they settled on DCB's "How He Loves" (it turned out that Tanni actually quite disliked the song, while James was long under the impression that she loved it). Over the course of the next year, both Tanni and James served in leadership for CFA and grew in mutual admiration, seeing the other's love for Jesus and care for others.

This admiration gradually grew into attraction which became solidified in commitment on March 31, 2013. James and Tanni greatly enjoyed their time dating, experiencing the ups and downs and ups of learning one another, meeting the other's family, and having adventures both together and at a distance from one another. However, this finally changed on a fateful Sunday in October 2014...

THE PROPOSAL

On the bright Sunday afternoon of October 5, Ashley asked Tanni to go to 86th Street with her to check out a cafe as a study break. Somewhat reluctantly, Tanni agreed to go. Little did she expect the surprise that would be waiting for her...

Several months earlier, James began plotting with Ashley and Lilian for that afternoon. Ashley had put together a lovely birthday celebration for Tanni in April, taking a group of us to Cafe Lalo, one of the main scenes from Tanni's favorite film, You've Got Mail. Wanting to continue with this same line of inspiration, James decided that their proposal would be a personal re-make of the movie's ending, where Tom Hanks's character asks Meg Ryan's character to meet in a particular spot of Riverside Park, coincidentally one of Tanni and James's favorite spots in the city, and reveals himself as her secret lover...

Once Tanni and Ashley emerged from the station at 86th Street, Ashley sent a text to James, who was already pre-positioned with Lilian beside The 91st Street Garden in Riverside Park. James then sent Tanni a text replicating Tom Hanks's message. With a little help from Ashley, Tanni then understood that this was no ordinary movie reference; the two headed towards the garden. After a short solo photoshoot, James emerged from his hidden spot in the wings with daisies and a poem he wrote in hand. He read, he knelt, he asked, and the two embraced as fiance and fiancee.

The day was wrapped up with a few more photos in the park, a quaint dinner at Jacob's Pickles, and exciting moments of calling friends and family to spread the good news.
